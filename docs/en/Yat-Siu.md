# Yat Siu

## Profile

Yat Siu is the Founder and Executive Chairman of Animoca Brands, an advocate for Web3 and the open metaverse.

|------|---------|
| **Position** | Founder & Executive Chairman |
| **Company** | [[Animoca-Brands]] |

| **Vision** | Drive digital ownership and build an open metaverse |

---

## Core Philosophy

Yat Siu frequently emphasizes the following concepts:

### Digital Ownership

- Users should truly own their digital assets
- Opposing the closed ecosystems of tech giants
- Supporting an open, interoperable metaverse

### Web3 Values

- Decentralization
- User sovereignty
- Open ecosystem

---

## 2026 Strategic Views

According to a November 2025 CNBC interview, Yat Siu and CSO Keyvan Peymani announced that Animoca will:

- Expand from gaming/metaverse to stablecoins, RWA, AI, and DePIN
- Maintain commitment to gaming ("stay true to its roots")
- Drive [[納斯達克上市|Nasdaq listing]]

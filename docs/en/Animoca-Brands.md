# Animoca Brands Company Overview

## Company Introduction

**Animoca Brands Corporation Limited** is a Hong Kong-based leader in digital assets, focusing on blockchain and tokenized assets to drive the future of Web3 innovation.

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Founded** | 2014 (originally a game developer) |
| **Headquarters** | Hong Kong |
| **Founder** | Yat Siu |
| **Core Mission** | Drive digital ownership and build an open metaverse |
| **2026 Strategy** | Stablecoins, RWA (Real World Assets), AI, DePIN |

## 2026 Strategic Transformation

At the end of 2025, Animoca Brands announced a major strategic transformation from a pure Web3 gaming investor to a **comprehensive digital asset conglomerate**.

### Four New Priority Areas

| Area | Description | Actions |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Stablecoins** | Compliant stablecoin issuance | Partnership with [[Standard-Chartered]] and [[HKT]] |
| **RWA** | Real World Asset tokenization | NUVA platform, [[Plume-Network]] partnership, [[Rayls]] partnership |
| **AI** | AI integration | Animoca Minds (with Ethoswarm) |
| **DePIN** | Decentralized Physical Infrastructure | Investing in related projects |

### Stablecoin Strategy

- **Partners:** Standard Chartered, HKT (Hong Kong Telecom)
- **Goal:** Launch compliant stablecoins
- **Use Cases:** Cross-border payments, Web3 gaming economy

### RWA (Real World Assets) Strategy

- **NUVA Platform:** Partnership with Provenance Blockchain Labs
- **[[Plume-Network]] Partnership:** Providing institutional-grade RWA yields for 700M+ users
- **[[Rayls]] Partnership:** Institutional-grade RWA tokenization infrastructure

### AI Strategy

- **Animoca Minds:** Launch of AI agent economy with Ethoswarm (officially released Feb 5, 2026)
- **Gaming AI:** NPC intelligence, procedural content generation

**Animoca Minds Details:**
- **Release Date:** Feb 5, 2026 (early access)
- **Partners:** Ethoswarm / CryptoSlam
- **Core Function:** Deploy persistent AI agents without hardware
- **User Interface:** Natural language interaction via email and Telegram
- **Blockchain Integration:** Supports identity, economy, and provenance layers

### Core Business Continuity

Despite the transformation, the following businesses continue:
- [[The-Sandbox]] — Metaverse platform
- [[Moca-Network]] — Digital identity (key focus)
- 600+ portfolio management
- Web3 gaming investments
